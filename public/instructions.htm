Decision-Making Experiment

This session is part of a study in decision making. You will be paid at the end of the session. The amount of money you earn will depend upon the decisions you make and on the decisions other people make.

The experiment consists of three separate rounds called MATCHES.

You will be assigned to be either the Red or the Blue player, and you will keep your role for all 3 matches.

Each match, you will be randomly and anonymously paired with a different player of the other role. You will never play again with the same player. Red moves first in the match, and has two choices: STOP or GO.
